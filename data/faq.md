### 1. Is this app giving me buy/sell signals?

No. This app is **only for education**.  
It explains basic concepts like PE, PB, candlesticks, support–resistance, forex pairs, pips, lots and risk management.

---

### 2. Can I use this to decide which stock to buy?

You **should not** rely on this app to decide what to buy or sell.  
Always do your own research, read company financials, and if needed, consult a qualified advisor.

---

### 3. What is the basic flow to start learning the Indian stock market?

1. Understand what NIFTY and SENSEX are.  
2. Learn basic terms: share, index, broker, Demat account.  
3. Learn **fundamental concepts** – PE, PB, EPS, ROE, debt, cash flow.  
4. Learn **technical basics** – support, resistance, candlestick chart, trend.  
5. Practice on a virtual or small-capital account rather than going all-in.

---

### 4. Is Forex trading legal in India?

Forex trading is regulated in India.  
Only certain **INR currency pairs** and exchanges are allowed under RBI and SEBI regulations when traded through authorized entities.

Be careful with:\n
- Unregulated offshore brokers\n
- Very high leverage and promises of guaranteed returns

Always check the latest regulations and consult official sources.

---

### 5. How should a beginner think about risk?

- Only use money that you can afford to lose.
- Avoid using loans or borrowed money to trade.
- Set a maximum loss per trade (e.g., 1–2% of total capital).
- Accept that losses are part of the learning process.
- Focus on **consistency and discipline**, not jackpot wins.
